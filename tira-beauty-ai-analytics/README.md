# AI-Driven Customer Analytics for Tira Beauty

## Business Objective

This project explores how artificial intelligence and analytics can improve customer engagement, personalization, and lead conversion for an online beauty retail platform.

## Case Study

Tira Beauty – a digital beauty retail platform.

## Analytical Applications

### 1. AI Chatbot

A chatbot concept designed to assist customers with product recommendations, FAQs, and personalized beauty guidance.

### 2. Product Recommendation System

A simple recommender system concept that suggests beauty products based on customer preferences and browsing behavior.

### 3. Sentiment Analysis

Customer reviews and feedback are analyzed to understand customer satisfaction and identify product improvement opportunities.

### 4. Feature Importance Analysis

Key factors influencing customer purchasing behavior are analyzed to understand which attributes drive conversions.

### 5. Lead Scoring Model

A basic lead scoring approach is used to identify high-potential customers based on behavioral indicators.

## Tools Used

Python
Business Analytics Techniques
AI-driven Customer Insights

## Outcome

The project demonstrates how AI and analytics can help beauty retail platforms improve personalization, marketing effectiveness, and customer experience.
